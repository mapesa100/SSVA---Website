About Us

SSVA's vision is to build a prosperous, peaceful, and united South Sudan where every citizen can thrive and contribute to the development of their community and country.
Our mission is to implement strategic actions that promote sustainable development, ensuring that every South Sudanese has access to basic services, opportunities for economic growth, and a voice in governance.

Our Core Values

Inclusivity

Prioritizing the participation of all communities, promoting gender equality and ensuring marginalized groups have a platform.

Integrity

Upholding transparency and accountability in all actions and decisions.

Collaboration

Fostering partnerships with government, NGOs and international organizations to maximize impact.

Sustainability

Committed to environmental preservation and economic resilience for future generations.

Strategic Goals

Economic Development

  1. Promote local entrepreneurship and small businesses.
  2. Support sustainable agriculture and natural resource management.
  3. Enhance infrastructure development, focusing on roads, energy, and telecommunications.

Education and Skills Development

  1. Increase access to quality education for all age groups.
  2. Establish vocational training programs to equip youth with the necessary skills.
  3. Promote adult education and literacy programs.

Health and Wellbeing

  1. Improve healthcare services and access to essential medicines.
  2. Implement programs for maternal and child health.
  3. Focus on disease prevention and health education initiatives.

Peace and Security

  1. Promote dialogue and reconciliation among communities to foster peace.
  2. Strengthen law enforcement and community safety programs.
  3. Support initiatives aimed at reducing violence and conflict.

Good Governance and Human Rights

  • Advocate for democratic governance and the rule of law.
  • Ensure citizens' rights are upheld and protected.
  • Promote civic engagement and participation in decision-making.

Implementation Strategies

Partnerships: We collaborate with local and international organizations to secure resources and funding.

Community Engagement: We involve communities in planning to ensure relevance and sustainability.

Monitoring & Evaluation: We regularly track progress and adapt our strategies for maximum impact.
